Homemade Basil Pesto

Cook time: 15 minutes

Level: Easy

Makes: 125g

Ingredients

  • 80g Basil
  • 50g Grated parmesan
  • 150ml Olive oil
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 50g Ground almonds
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt & pepper

Method

    Step One

  • Blend the basil in a food processor. Then add the parmesan and ground almonds.

    • Step Two

  • Slowly add the olive oil whilst continuing to blend in intervals. Do the same with the lemon juice, adding a little bit at a time to suit your taste.

    • Step Three

  • Once it starts to form a paste consistency, add the garlic and season to taste.
